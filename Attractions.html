ATTRACTIONS

Left Shores

BAITEREK

Baiterek is a monument in the shape of a golden ball on top of a 97-meter tower. It was built in 1996-2002 and is one of the most recognizable symbols of the country.

KHAN SHATYR

Khan Shatyr is the world's largest tent-shaped entertainment center. It was designed by British architect Norman Foster and opened in 2010.

EXPO

Expo 2017 is an international specialized exhibition that took place from June 10 to September 10, 2017. The theme of the exhibition is “Future Energy”.

Right Shores

PALACE OF PEACE

The Palace of Peace and Reconciliation is a unique pyramid-shaped structure. It was built in 2006 according to the design of British architect Norman Foster.

NATIONAL MUSEUM

The National Museum is the largest museum in Kazakhstan. It was opened in 2014 and is part of the architectural ensemble of Independence Square.

MONUMENT KAZAKH ELI

The Kazakh Eli monument is a sculptural composition. It was opened in 2010 and is part of the architectural ensemble of Independence Square.
