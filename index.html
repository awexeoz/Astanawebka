Interesting facts

1. Nur-Sultan, formerly Astana, was renamed in honor of the first president of Kazakhstan, Nursultan Nazarbayev, in March 2019.

2. This city is famous for its modern architecture, including the symbol of the city - the Baiterek monument.

3. Nur-Sultan is the political and administrative center of Kazakhstan.

4. The city is located in the center of the steppes, which creates amazing natural landscapes around it.

5. Nur-Sultan attracts people from a variety of ethnic groups and languages, including Russian, Kazakh and English.
